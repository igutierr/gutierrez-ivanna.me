Welcome to GitHub Pages.

My dream since elementary school has been to work with computers. Currently I am a undergraduate student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, majoring in Software Information Systems with a concentration in Web Development and a minor in Mathematics. I have worked at the Game+Learning lab at UNC Charlotte creating games that teach computer science concepts to children. I also volunteer at the Microsoft Digigirlz Summer Program. There I helpd young girls learn more about technology and career possibilities in these fields. In 2013 and 2014 I coordinated summer camps for middle school girls called "Girls & Tech" from the Aspire IT NCWIT grant. These camps focused on using Kodu, CS Unplugged, and Khan Academy to teach computer science concepts. I expect to continue coordinating these summer camps in coming years. During the summer of 2013 and 2014 I also interned at Bank of America as a Global Market Risk Technology and Operations (GMRT&O) Developer intern. I also like to help teach children how to read and write in Spanish using bible stories.
